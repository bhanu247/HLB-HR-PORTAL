Other Services

Dedicated HR Partner

A Dedicated HR Partner refers to the provision of a specialized HR professional or team assigned specifically to work closely with an organization or a specific department within it. This partner acts as a primary point of contact for all HR-related matters, offering personalized guidance, support, and strategic HR advice tailored to the unique needs and goals of the organization. This service ensures that the organization receives focused attention, facilitating quick and effective resolution of HR issues, strategic workforce planning, and enhanced HR service delivery that aligns with the organization's objectives.

Employee File Management

Employee File Management involves the systematic organization, storage, and maintenance of employee-related documents and records. This service encompasses the management of a wide range of documents, including employment contracts, performance reviews, training records, personal information, and compliance documentation. Effective employee file management ensures that all vital records are securely stored, easily accessible, and maintained in compliance with legal and regulatory requirements. This not only aids in efficient HR operations and decision-making but also supports privacy, confidentiality, and data protection practices within the organization.
