HR Outsourcing Services

Payroll Processing Services

Payroll processing services manage all aspects of employee compensation, including calculating wages, withholding taxes, and ensuring timely payments. This service ensures accuracy and compliance with tax laws, reducing the administrative burden on businesses.

HR Consulting Services

HR consulting services offer expert advice on various HR practices and strategies, helping businesses optimize their human resource management. This can include organizational structure, performance management, and employee development programs.

Emiratization Regulations Advisory

Emiratization regulations advisory focuses on helping businesses understand and comply with policies aimed at increasing the employment of local citizens in the UAE. This service ensures that companies meet government quotas and benefit from incentives.

Government Department Liaising

This service involves acting as an intermediary between the company and government departments, handling all necessary communications, applications, and paperwork for compliance and regulatory purposes.

Benefits Administration

This includes Social Security and Pension Contributions, Benefit Enrollment such as health insurance, life insurance, and more, Employee End of Service Benefits.

Recruitment Process Outsourcing(RPO)

RPO involves delegating the entire recruitment process to an external provider, from job posting to candidate selection. This service helps businesses streamline their hiring process, ensuring access to a wider talent pool and better candidate fits.

Employee onboarding/Off Boarding

Provides clear protocols for hiring and terminating employees, ensuring legal compliance and smooth transitions, Assists in obtaining necessary work permits for employees, ensuring compliance with immigration laws, Conducts thorough checks on potential hires to verify their backgrounds and suitability for the role.

Employee Contracts & Employment Administration

Manages all aspects of employee contracts, from creation to administration, ensuring compliance with labor laws and internal policies. This service also includes managing other employment-related documentation and processes.

Visa Advisory Services

Offers guidance on visa application processes for employees, ensuring compliance with immigration laws and regulations. This service is crucial for businesses with international workforces.

PRO Services

Provides Public Relations Officer (PRO) services to handle all government-related paperwork and procedures, streamlining the process for businesses and ensuring timely compliance.

Medical fitness test

Coordinates and manages medical fitness tests required for employment, ensuring that all employees meet the health standards required by local regulations.
